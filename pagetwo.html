A playlist for you, based on your sign

Most of us know our sun sign. You could be a talkative gemini, or a snarky capricorn — or any other sign in between. I took what I know of the stars and my love for music and made 12 different playlists, one for each sign. I don't have an extensive playlist methodology, just vibes. I hope you enjoy — but if you're a virgo please keep the criticism to yourself, thanks!

For the purposes of the assignment, for my second and final draft I want people to be able to choose their sign and then the playlist will show up, but for now I'm just displaying the playlists. Also I plan on adding more songs to each playlist- this is just a start.
